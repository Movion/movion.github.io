SWMC tos

The Starwars MC server (SWMC) staff team reserves to change the following terms at any point without notice. By accepting the ingame prompt upon entering the game, each user agrees to follow to the rules set upon the server, and accepts that they fully acknowledge and understand the following:

collection of data

To provide players with the most enjoyable time on our server we require users to consent the following collection of data associated with their minecraft account including but not limited to:

• IP addresses and login information
• Ingame stats
• Rank and purchase details
• Ingame chat and command logs
• Ingame and chat behavior

Data collected will not be sold to third parties and will solely be used to ensure everyone has an enjoyable playing experience.

donations and purchases

The SWMC staff reserve the right to change donation perks at any time. If a user is banned from entering our server, their donator rank may be forfeit and they may not be refunded. There is no guarantee on being able to join the server, and during the event that the server is no longer operated, virtual ranks or purchases are forfeit. Refund requests due to issues concerning lag, game glitches, or other issues are under the discretion of the SWMC administration team.

ingame rules and punishments

Bans and punishments for breaking rules are under the full discretion of the SWMC administration and moderation teams. If a user has a problem or concern with a punishment, they may appeal and/or talk to the staff member who punished them. The SWMC staff team reserves the right to change rules at any time without notice.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding these terms, please talk to a SWMC staff member or email us at info@starwarsmc.org
